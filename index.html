About Design with Kad.

Design with Kad is an initiative to help young individuals start and uplift their graphic design career at the Federal University of Technology, Akure. We are starting with a small number of inquisitive individuals. Let's see what the future holds.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is the course really free?
+

Design with Kad is completely free.

What should I bring to class?
+

Your laptop or Smartphone, a notebook, and an open mind ready to learn.

Do I need prior design experience?
+

Yes and No. If you're a beginner, we would start from the fundamentals and progressively build your skill. If you already have prior design experience, the Design with Kad session would work for you.

What software will we use?
+

We would be using softwares like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma and a couple of others. Smartphone Designers would be allowed to use their phones.

When are the classes held?
+

Sundays at IFT Lecture Room, School of Computing, FUTA, Akure.
