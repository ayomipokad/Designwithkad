Frequently Asked Questions
Is the course really free?
+

Yes! Design with Kad. is completely free. We believe quality design education should be accessible to everyone passionate about learning.

What should I bring to class?
+

Bring your laptop, a notebook for sketching ideas, and an open mind ready to learn. We'll provide all the software guidance you need.

Do I need prior design experience?
+

Not at all! We welcome complete beginners. Our curriculum starts from the fundamentals and progressively builds your skills.

What software will we use?
+

We primarily use industry-standard tools like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Figma. Free alternatives will also be discussed for those who need them.

When are the classes held?
+

Every Sunday at IFT Lecture Room, School of Computing, FUTA, Akure. Check our outline page for specific times and lesson schedules.

Outline

Explore our comprehensive curriculum. Each lesson builds on the previous one, taking you from design fundamentals to advanced techniques.

Lesson 1: Introduction to Graphic Design

+

Discover the fundamentals of graphic design, including design principles, color theory basics, and an overview of essential design software. We'll explore what makes great design and set up your creative workspace.

Lesson 2: Typography Essentials

+

Master the art of typography. Learn about font families, hierarchy, spacing, and how to choose the perfect typeface for your projects. Understand the psychology behind different font styles.

Lesson 3: Color Theory Deep Dive

+

Explore advanced color theory concepts, including color harmony, contrast, and emotional impact. Learn to create stunning color palettes and understand color psychology in design.

Lesson 4: Layout and Composition

+

Study the principles of effective layout design. Master grid systems, visual hierarchy, balance, and alignment to create professional, eye-catching compositions.

Lesson 5: Logo Design Principles

+

Learn the art of logo design from concept to execution. Understand brand identity, symbolism, and how to create memorable, versatile logos that stand the test of time.

Lesson 6: Adobe Photoshop Fundamentals

+

Get hands-on with Photoshop. Master essential tools, layers, masks, and basic photo manipulation techniques. Learn to retouch images and create digital artwork.

Lesson 7: Adobe Illustrator Basics

+

Discover vector graphics with Illustrator. Learn pen tool mastery, shape building, and creating scalable designs perfect for logos, icons, and illustrations.

Lesson 8: Brand Identity Design

+

Create comprehensive brand identities. Learn to develop brand guidelines, design business cards, letterheads, and maintain visual consistency across all brand touchpoints.

Lesson 9: Social Media Design

+

Master social media graphics. Design engaging posts, stories, and banners optimized for various platforms. Learn about platform specifications and viral design principles.

Lesson 10: Poster and Flyer Design

+

Create impactful promotional materials. Learn composition techniques for print design, understand print specifications, and design posters that grab attention.

Lesson 11: UI/UX Design Basics

+

Introduction to digital product design. Understand user experience principles, wireframing, prototyping, and creating intuitive user interfaces with Figma.

Lesson 12: Portfolio Building & Career Development

+

Build a professional portfolio that showcases your best work. Learn how to present your projects, network with clients, and launch your design career or freelance business.
